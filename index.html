Privacy Policy

Data Collection

Hawk Bot does not store personal user data. We only access message and user data in real-time to provide moderation and protection features. No data is permanently stored on external servers.

How We Use Information

Information such as usernames, message content, and server metadata is used temporarily to enforce anti-raid systems, moderation rules, and bot responses. None of this information is shared or sold.

Third-Party Access

Hawk does not share your information with any third-party service. We do not use any analytics or tracking software. Everything runs locally within the Discord API scope.

Your Rights

You may remove Hawk from your server at any time. Doing so will immediately stop any interaction and data access. No further data is stored or retrievable after removal.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may change over time. Continued use of Hawk after changes implies acceptance of the updated policy. Please check back periodically for revisions.
